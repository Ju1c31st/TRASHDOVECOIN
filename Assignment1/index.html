DoveCoin is an open source peer-2-peer currency of digital sense, favored by Doves worldwide.

How does it work?

DoveCoin is a public blockchain which is a decentralised cryptocurrency project and it's technology is available through open source. DoveCoin is being worked on by smart people to develop a smart contract platform which delivers advanced features never seen before. This is the first cryptocurrency which has evolved out of years of scientific philosophy and a research driven approach. The team which develops this is a large global gathering of expert engineers and researchers.
